Tomato Avocado Sandwich

A vegan-friendly easy-to-make yummy sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Â½ avocado - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 slice ciabatta bread
  • 3 slices tomato
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Mix mashed avocado and garlic salt together in a bowl; spread onto ciabatta bread. Layer tomatoes over avocado and top with black pepper.
